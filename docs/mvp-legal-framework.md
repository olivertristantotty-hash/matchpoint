## 1. Disclaimers

These must be visible in the Discord welcome channel, the website footer, and the Terms of Service. Not buried — prominent.

### Platform Classification
> MATCHPOINT is a skill-based competitive gaming platform. All competitions on MATCHPOINT are contests of skill where the outcome is determined by the participants' ability in the chosen video game. MATCHPOINT does not offer, facilitate, or promote games of chance, lotteries, or casino-style gambling.

### Eligibility
> You must be at least 18 years old to participate in real-money competitions on MATCHPOINT. By creating an account and participating, you confirm that you meet this age requirement and that skill-based gaming competitions are permitted in your jurisdiction. MATCHPOINT is not available to residents of jurisdictions where skill-based competitions for prizes are prohibited by law.

### Risk Acknowledgment
> All participation in real-money competitions is voluntary. There is always a risk of loss. Entry fees are non-refundable once a match begins. Never stake more than you can afford to lose. Past performance does not guarantee future results.

### Payout Terms
> All payouts are subject to result verification and the platform's Terms of Service. Match results are final once confirmed through the settlement process. A 10% platform fee is deducted from the prize pool of every settled real-money competition.

## 2. Communication Tone

### Language Rules for All Platform Communications

**Always say:**
- "Compete for the prize pool"
- "Prove your skill"
- "Challenge your opponent"
- "Skill-based competition"
- "Entry stake" or "match stake"
- "Prize pool" or "winnings"
- "You have the opportunity to win"

**Never say:**
- "Bet" / "betting" / "gamble" / "gambling"
- "You will win" / "guaranteed win"
- "Casino" / "odds" / "house edge"
- "Jackpot" / "payout" (use "prize" or "winnings")
- "Lucky" / "luck-based"

### Event Announcements — Always Show the Math
Every wager and competition must clearly display:
- The stake per player
- The total prize pool
- The platform fee
- The winner's take

Example: "500 tokens each → 1,000 token pool → 100 fee → Winner takes 900 tokens"

This is already built into every wager card and thread in the bot.

## 3. Moderation & Enforcement

### Tiered Punishment System

| Offense | Action | Details |
|---------|--------|---------|
| First no-show | -10 rep + 1 strike | Didn't report result in time |
| Second no-show | -10 rep + 1 strike | Pattern of abandoning matches |
| Third no-show | Permanent ban | 3 strikes = automatic ban |
| Fake result / forged evidence | Instant permanent ban | Zero tolerance. All linked accounts blacklisted. |
| Collusion | Both accounts permanently banned | Detected via alternating-win pattern analysis |
| Alt account | All accounts permanently banned | Detected via linked game account cross-reference |
| Harassment / abuse | Warning → kick → ban | Escalating based on severity |
| Underage user | Account locked immediately | Required to verify age to unlock |

### Rule Acknowledgment
Users must interact with the setup-guide channel (clicking buttons to link accounts, etc.) before they can participate in real-money competitions. This serves as implicit acknowledgment of the rules and disclaimers.

### Dispute Resolution
1. Both players submit their result independently (blind)
2. If results agree → auto-settled
3. If results conflict → dispute opened
4. Moderator reviews screenshot evidence in the match thread
5. Player without screenshot evidence loses the dispute
6. Player found to have submitted fake evidence → instant permanent ban
7. Moderator decision is final
